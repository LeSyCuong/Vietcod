We are looking for a Design Engineer with refined taste to help evolve the Unkey product, and brand. We believe code is a tool for designers to solve problems, just like Figma. We want to work with experienced individuals who can take on any challenge and solve it at speed and scale.

## In this role you will...

- Explore designs in Figma and translate it to web-native experiences
- You will be responsible for building complex and interactive UI web/app experiences
- Prototype ideas quickly to test concepts and push the product forward
- Translate customer requests into solutions

## You will be a great fit if you...

- Are great at problem solving
- Can clearly communicate your ideas
- You care about a polished product
- Jump in the code to polish and improve the experience with autonomy

## What it means to join the team:

- Autonomy to "just ship it"
- 100% remote team with flexible working schedules
- Modern tech stack (TailwindCSS, React, Next.js)
- Honest and low-ego team
- Ownership of problems and solutions
